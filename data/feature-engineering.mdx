## Clinical Feature Engineering

Transform raw EHR data into meaningful predictive features:

- Temporal aggregations (mean, trend, variance)
- Derived risk scores
- Comorbidity indices
- Medication patterns

## Computer Vision Features

Extract rich representations from food images:

- Deep learned embeddings (ViT features)
- Nutritional composition vectors
- Portion size estimation
- Food category hierarchies
