## Literature Review

### Deep Learning for Diabetes Prediction

Recent advances in neural networks for T2DM risk prediction:

1. **Recurrent Networks**: LSTM/GRU for temporal EHR data
2. **Attention Mechanisms**: Identify critical features
3. **Transfer Learning**: Leverage pre-trained models
4. **Multimodal Learning**: Combine clinical + imaging data

### Computer Vision for Nutrition

Food recognition and dietary assessment:

1. **CNN-based**: ResNet, EfficientNet for classification
2. **Transformers**: ViT, DETR for food detection
3. **Portion Estimation**: Depth estimation, reference objects
4. **Nutritional Databases**: USDA FoodData Central integration
