## Healthcare AI Applications

Machine learning is transforming healthcare:

- **Diagnostic Imaging**: Radiology, pathology
- **Risk Prediction**: Disease onset, progression
- **Treatment Optimization**: Precision medicine
- **Clinical Decision Support**: EHR integration

## Challenges

- Data privacy and security
- Model interpretability
- Clinical validation
- Regulatory approval
- Bias and fairness
