## Study Design

### Data Sources

1. **MIMIC-IV**: Clinical risk modeling (N=50,048 patients)
2. **MM-Food-100K**: Food recognition training (N=100,000 images)

### Train/Validation/Test Split

- Training: 70%
- Validation: 15%
- Test: 15%

Stratified by diabetes status to preserve class distribution.

### Model Training

- **Risk Predictor**: Bidirectional LSTM
- **Food Analyzer**: Vision Transformer (ViT-B/16)
- **Recommendation Engine**: Hybrid rule-based + RL

### Evaluation Metrics

- AUROC, AUPRC
- Sensitivity, Specificity
- Calibration (ECE, Brier score)
- Feature importance (SHAP)
