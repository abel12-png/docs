## Multi-Task Learning

Simultaneously predict:
- Food category (classification)
- Portion size (regression)
- Macronutrients (multi-output regression)

## Performance

- Calorie MAE: 42.5 kcal
- Protein MAE: 3.2g
- Carbs MAE: 5.8g
- Fat MAE: 2.1g
