## Risk-Stratified Recommendations

Tailor nutrition advice to individual risk profiles:

- **Low Risk**: General healthy eating guidelines
- **Moderate Risk**: Enhanced glycemic control strategies
- **High Risk**: Intensive dietary intervention
- **Very High Risk**: Medical nutrition therapy

## Recommendation Algorithm

Combines:
- Clinical risk score
- Current dietary intake (from food images)
- Nutritional guidelines (ADA, WHO)
- Personal preferences and constraints
